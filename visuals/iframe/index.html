Embedding Video

Use include-iframe to embed content from other place. By default, aspect ratio set to 16:9 . :include-iframe: https://www.youtube.com/embed/tgbNymZ7vqY

Aspect Ratio

Use aspectRatio to change aspect ratio of the embedded content :include-iframe: https://www.youtube.com/embed/tgbNymZ7vqY {aspectRatio: "4:3"}
